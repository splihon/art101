Baking a Cake

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Gather Ingredients:
    • Flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, eggs, milk, oil, vanilla extract
  3. Mix Dry Ingredients:
    • Flour, Sugar, Baking Powder, and Salt in a large mixing bowl
    • Mix thoroughly to blend ingredients together
  4. Mix Wet Ingredients:
    • In another bowl, crack the eggs
    • Add milk, oil, and vanilla extract
    • Whisk until the mixture is smooth
  5. Combine Wet and Dry ingredients:
    • Pour the wet mixture into the bowl with the dry ingredients
    • Mix until the batter is smooth and there are no lumps
  6. Prepare Baking Pan:
    • Grease the baking pan with non-stick spray
  7. Pour batter into Pan
  8. Bake the Cake:
    • Place the pan in the preheated oven
    • Bake for 25 to 30 minutes
  9. Remove Cake from the Oven
